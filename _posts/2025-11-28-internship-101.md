On November 25th, SKIP hosted an internship workshop event to aid attendees in finding their dream internships in the tech industry.

The event started with an insightful presentation from HR professional and our fellow classmate [Lamia Nabil](https://se.linkedin.com/in/lamianabil), showing attendees key points of improving their resume, some common mistakes when constructing a CV and general interview advice.

![CV improvement presentation picture 1](/assets/posts/internship-101-2025/cv-improvement-presentation-1.JPG)
![CV improvement presentation picture 2](/assets/posts/internship-101-2025/cv-improvement-presentation-2.JPG)

What followed was a Q&A session with former student interns, 3rd year SEM students: [Maxine Orlen](https://se.linkedin.com/in/maxine-orlen), [Razvan Albu](https://se.linkedin.com/in/albu-razvan) and [Bimnet Tesfamariam](https://se.linkedin.com/in/bimnet-tesfamariam), where the interns conveyed their experiences, their journey acquiring the internship and some general tips to stand out to recruiters.

![Previous interns](/assets/posts/internship-101-2025/previous-interns.JPG)

We hope the advice from the event will aid you in landing your dream internships! Thank you for attending.
