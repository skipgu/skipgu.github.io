Below you can find a brief summary of the thrilling activities and achievements of SKIP during the academic year 2025/2026. You can find a more thorough report **[here](/assets/posts/activity-report-2025-2026/report.pdf)**.

## Internship 101

We've organised an insightful session on students' internship experiences within the tech industry. Students were given tips on how to enhance their resumes by a professional HR, had a Q&A with her and 3rd years about their intership experiences and how they got them.

Attendees left with a better understanding of applying to the tech industry and what they can expect during their internships.

![Internship 101](/assets/posts/activity-report-2025-2026/intership-101.png)

## THE INTERNSHIP EXPERIENCE

To inspire and motivate aspiring software engineers, SKIP hosted a session with an employee from Amazon Web Services (AWS). The employee presented steps towards applying for an internship at AWS as well as their experience working at AWS. The session provided a clear and informative view into Big Tech for the members of the session.

![THE INTERNSHIP EXPERIENCE](/assets/posts/activity-report-2025-2026/intership-experience.png)

## Create something Lovable - Hackathon

SKIP, in partnership with **Lovable**, launched a three-day hackathon focused on building "Minimum Lovable Products". The event, fueled by **Red Bull**, had contestants brainstorming creative solutions for the UI/UX and Innovation & Originality categories! Their following MVPs were presented to our judges from **GU Ventures**, **the University of Gothenburg** and **Chalmers Technical University** so that they could receive valuable feedback. Detailed information can be seen in the [blog post](https://skipgu.org/2026/05/23/build-something-lovable-hackathon.html)!

![Photo during hackathon](/assets/posts/activity-report-2025-2026/build-something-lovable-hackathon.PNG)

## Online Presence, Communication, and Social Media

We are continuing to further develop our website which provides information about the organisation and its events. In order to promote the visibility of the website, promotional material such a posters and social media posts (shared on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/skip-student-association), [Discord](https://tinyurl.com/skip-discord) and [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/skip.gu/)) were created and displayed. We hope to further enhance the experience of using our website in the upcoming months!

Overall, the academic year 2025/2026 was filled with successful events and activities in order to support the academic growth for all involved. SKIP will continue in promoting a professional, safe and enriching environment to the academic community.
